My story

I will try to briefly tell you my story without boring you too much...

I was born in Rome, Italy in 1985 and now I live in Sora (FR). Since I was a child I was attracted by technology, I was fascinated by automatisms, so in choosing which high school to attend, the most obvious decision for me was the technical institute with a specialization in computer science. I was thrilled by what I could create, by the automatisms and interactions triggered by simple inputs.

Unfortunately, I already knew that after school, the family business awaited me (and it had nothing to do with computers). In order not to forget what I had learned, I started practicing on my own with my computer, I also developed a small management software for the family business for fun, but obviously my project could not replace a management software paid thousands of euros and that the staff already knew how to use. Seeing that my commitment was not appreciated and not knowing where to direct it, I stopped practicing and resigned myself to the idea that my future would lie in that company, until about eight years later when I decided to take other paths.

By now what I had studied was obsolete, and in any case, even looking back at the books, I remembered almost nothing. It seemed to me that I had "missed the train", and I began to carry out the most disparate jobs, but inside of me I knew that I still had the passion and predisposition for technology, and that I was wasting time and energy on activities jobs that I didn't like and didn't reward me. Even those who know me well knew it, so they started encouraging me to go back to computer science.

In 2023 I finally decided to upgrade with current web development technologies, and I chose Start2Impact University for that purpose. Today I am determined to put my knowledge at the service of others and to do what I like most.
